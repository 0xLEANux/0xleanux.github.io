FAQ

Which method is best?

If you want to support long-term work, GitHub Sponsors is the best default. For one-time support, Card (Stripe) or PayPal is simplest. Crypto is fine if you prefer it.

Can I donate to both DEV and ORG?

Yes. Use the offerings section (it shows DEV and ORG buttons side by side). You can also switch the recipient in the top bar.

Will I get a receipt?

Receipts depend on the provider. Card/PayPal providers typically provide proof of payment. Sponsors provides a record via GitHub.

Why is Stripe/PayPal not directly in GitHub’s Sponsor dropdown?

GitHub’s sponsor button supports specific platforms and custom URLs. For Stripe/PayPal/crypto, a custom URL (this page) is the standard solution.
